1. Type of Insurance
Product: Jewellery & Watch Insurance – All Risks Cover
Description: This policy provides worldwide cover for accidental damage, mechanical breakdown, theft and loss of specified jewellery and watches. Cover applies anywhere in the world, subject to policy terms.
2. Insured Item(s)
3. What is Insured? (Summary of Cover)
  • Accidental Damage: Repair or replacement of the item if accidentally damaged (e.g. knocked, dropped, crushed).
  • Theft: Replacement if stolen. A valid police report and crime reference number must be provided.
  • Loss (proven theft): Replacement where loss is evidenced as theft (police report required).
  • Mechanical Breakdown: Repair or replacement of watches for mechanical failure (subject to policy limits).
  • Worldwide Cover: Insured anywhere in the world, subject to policy terms and exclusions.
4. What is Not Insured? (Main Exclusions)
  • Loss not proved as theft: Unexplained disappearance, misplacement or loss where theft cannot be evidenced.
  • Wear and tear: Gradual deterioration, scratching, or damage from normal use.
  • Unattended items: Theft from an unattended vehicle or from a location where the item was left unattended (e.g. public changing room, gym locker).
  • Warranty/servicing: Damage covered by manufacturer warranty or due to routine servicing or repair.
  • Intentional acts: Damage or loss caused deliberately by the policyholder or a member of their household.
  • War, terrorism, confiscation: Loss or damage arising from war, terrorism, civil unrest, or confiscation by authorities.
  • Unspecified items: Items not listed on the policy schedule or exceeding the sum insured.
5. Policy Limits
Maximum cover per item: £0
Total sum insured: £0
Excess (deductible): £50 per claim (or as stated in policy schedule).
6. Obligations at Contract Start
  • You must be aged 18 or over and resident in the United Kingdom.
  • Provide accurate information about the insured item(s), including description, value and proof of purchase/valuation.
  • Declare any previous claims, losses or refusals of insurance.
  • Ensure items are stored securely when not in use (e.g. safe, locked cabinet).
7. Obligations During the Contract Term
  • Notify us of any change in circumstances that may affect the risk (e.g. change of address, new high-value item).
  • Keep items in secure storage when not worn; do not leave unattended in vehicles or public places.
  • Maintain proof of purchase and valuation documents.
8. Obligations When Claiming
  • Report loss/theft within 24 hours to the police and to us.
  • Obtain a police report and crime reference number for theft or attempted theft.
  • Provide: proof of purchase, valuation certificate, photographs, and any other evidence requested.
  • Cooperate fully with our claims investigation.
