Actuarial Risk Model

Competitive pricing: Pure Premium × (1 + expense) × (1 + profit) × risk factors

Model overview

Pure Premium = E[Loss] = λ × value × severityFactor × limitFactor × durationFactor × riskAppetite
λ = loss frequency (claims per 1000 exposures, by scenario)
severityFactor = expected loss % of value (power law)
limitFactor = coverage/value ratio
durationFactor = 1 + (days/365)×0.5
Gross Premium = Pure × (1 + 25% expense) × (1 + 12–20% profit) × 70% actuarial + 30% provider base
